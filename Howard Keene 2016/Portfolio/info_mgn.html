Having created a website for author Martin Naylor's first book, The Secrets of Hightower, I was asked to supply him with a second website, under his name. Using similar techniques, including a Flash Actionscript based ebook resembling an eReader. This second website gave Martin the ability to sell his books online, each transaction sending an email to Martin with the order details on it and a copy to the purchaser. Tests using a sandbox were successfully transferred to live Paypal. The site also linked back to the original secretsofhightower.org
