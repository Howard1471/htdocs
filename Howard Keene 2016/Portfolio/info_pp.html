Constructed as a prototype during my time as an intern consultant, with Nottingham Trent University, This single page website demonstrated an algorithm being used to define the personal characteristics of the user. The application guides the user through a series of scenarios for which they must indicate the most like them and least like them statements. The final outcome is displayed either as a bargraph, of the four main characteristics, or a four-axis graph with the outcome plotted on it.
