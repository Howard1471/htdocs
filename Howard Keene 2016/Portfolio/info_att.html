Motorcycle trials team, Ace Trials Team, required a website to attract potential sponsors to support their cause, to provide a team environment for young aspiring British Trials riders in World and European competition. The site included flash animations on the opening page and the home page header.

After each event the Team Secretary would present me with a disk of photos to include in the website. These were edited for size and imported in to the Gallery where each photo could be selected from a thumbnail and viewed in it's own page.

At it's peak, the site had in excess of 5000 individual pages. The site closed with the demise of the team.
