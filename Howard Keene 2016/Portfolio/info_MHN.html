This website was created to showcase the owners childcare nursery. Clients of the business were able to leave feedback for other visitors to read. These comments would remain hidden until the nursery owner released them. The large amount of information the owner wanted to display required a flash animation with Actionscript to 'sweep' pages in to and out of the screen area.
