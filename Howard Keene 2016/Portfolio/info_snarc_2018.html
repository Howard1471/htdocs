Designed to replace the club's previous website and update the framework, the South Notts ARC website was moved to a WordPress Framework. To make things easy for the committee to update for themselves, plugins were employed to display articles in PDF format and collect visitor enquiries through a mail form. News items are displayed in a headlines list which links to each story individually. The style theme uses the WordPress Twenty Eleven theme as this is the simplest format, to accomodate older users and maintain readability.
