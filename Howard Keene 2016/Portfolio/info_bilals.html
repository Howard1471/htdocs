Bilals Restaurant is an Indian Restaurant and Take-Away. The owner responded to a flyer I placed through his door in an attempt to attract business. The design reflected, as closely as possible, the restaurant menus to continue the colour scheme. The full take away menu was reproduced in the site. To make updating easier I used a VBA macro to convert an Excel spreadsheet into the required HTML to copy and paste into the page.

The sister site .co.uk comprised of a single page to redirect any visitor to this site automatically.
