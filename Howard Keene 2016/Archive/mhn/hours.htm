Hours of opening & Fees

 

We are open Monday to Friday between the hours of 7.30 a.m. to 6.30 p.m. through out the year excluding bank holidays.

* Fees are available on application.
* Full payment will be required for all Bank Holidays and any period of Sickness
* Fees are inclusive of all meal snacks drinks and nappies. (A charge of £2 will be added for lunch and £1.50 for tea if required with sessional care)
* 5% discount available for the oldest child if more than one child per family attends nursery.
* Discount offered for full time bookings.
* Two weeks holiday may be taken free of charge in one year i.e. if your child attends 4 sessions a week they will be entitled to 8 free sessions in a year. This can only apply if 4 weeks written notice is given to the nursery in order that staffing ratios are maintained. If your child attends for less than one year, then holidays will be pro rata.
* We will try to accommodate extra hours or sessions as required.
* Nursery fees are due from the day that your child starts the nursery. If your child starts at the nursery part way through a month, we will invoice you in advance for the period up to the end of the month.
* Nursery fees are payable per calendar month in advance on the first of each month. (E.g. 12 payments per year of the same amount).
