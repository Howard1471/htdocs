Dear Parent/Carer,

Welcome to the Manor House Nursery and Pre school

The Manor House Nursery provides high quality childcare for children aged 3 months to 8 years. We provide Breakfast and After-school clubs for children attending local schools and a Holiday club for the weeks when they don't attend their normal establishments.
The nursery is a bright, spacious and a stimulating Environment where children are encouraged to learn through play. The building is set in beautiful gardens giving your child ample opportunity to explore and enjoy having fun.

We recognize the early experiences in a Childs life will affect how they develop. it is of utmost importance that we ensure your child receives the love, security, stability, positive self image and respect he/she deserves.

It is the policy of our organisation to meet the needs and abilities of all children who attend by promoting opportunities for challenge, success independence in a safe caring environment. As parents you are integral to that success. In partnership we can provide the best possible learning experience for your child.

I take this opportunity to welcome you to visit our setting and arrange a suitable appointment for you and your child.
