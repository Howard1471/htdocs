Conditions of charging

The term "client" refers to the client or their representative in all cases below.
 

  1. All call outs must be paid for at the rate specified in the current published rates guide. This guide is published within this website.
  2. Call outs cannot be cancelled within 4 hours of the agreed timeslot.
  3. If the engineer finds that the client is unavailable then a full charge will be raised for one hour.
  4. If the engineer is unable to locate the clients premises or dwelling due to insufficient directions then a full charge will be raised for one hour.
  5. if the engineer is unable to fix the problem due to insufficient technical information then only the call out fee will be chargeable.
  6. If the engineer is able to provide a repair/fix but is prevented from doing so by the client then the client is liable for the full charges at the time of that intervention. See item 1.
  7. If the engineer is able to provide a repair/fix but is prevented from doing so because the client does not possess the necessary product keys or passwords then the client is liable for the full charges at the time of that issue. See item 1.
  8. If the engineer is able to provide a hardware repair/fix but is prevented from doing so because the client chooses not to proceed then the client is liable for the full charges at the time of that issue. See item 1.
  9. The client is responsible for the costs of any parts required to effect a repair/fix. A deposit fee will be required before parts will be ordered. The engineer will advise the client when the parts are available. The cost of the part will be deducted from the deposit.
  10. Virus, Spyware, adware and other malicious software issues are resolved on a call out, time basis and carry no guarantee of success due to the nature of these issues.
