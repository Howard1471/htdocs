Domain Registration is the process whereby you register your internet website name.

 

It's a bit like registering your car.  For a set fee, your name is reserved for your use and your use only.  Nobody else can use what you have registered, just like only you having a particular registration number.

Website names come with a variety of domain extensions such as .co.uk or .com

The same website name can exist if it has a different extension i.e. website.co.uk and website.com are two completely separate websites.  Not for profit organisations sometimes use .org or .net.  There are plenty to choose from.
